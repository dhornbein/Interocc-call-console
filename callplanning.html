HOW TO REGISTER A NICKNAME FOR THE FIRST TIME:
1. Enter this text into the entry field in the chat window to select a nickname:
/nick YourNickname
2. Next, enter this text with your selected password and email address:
/msg nickserv register your_password your_email_address
Check your email, and follow the instructions that are sent to you.

HOW TO USE A NICKNAME THAT YOU HAVE PREVIOUSLY SET UP:
1. When entering the chat, enter your nickname
2. Within 30 seconds, enter the following:
/msg nickserv identify YourNickname YourPassword
